Q) Solve the following quadratic equations. \\

1) $x^2+2x-15=0$ \\
Solution: \\
$x^2+5x-3x-15=0$ \\
$x(x+5)-3(x+5)=0$ \\
$(x+5)(x-3)=0$ \\
$(x+5)=0$ or $(x-3)=0$ \\
\textbf{x = -5} or \textbf{x = 3} \\
\\\\\\

2) $3x^2-5x+2=0$ \\
Solution: \\
$3x^2-3x-2x+2=0$ \\
$3x(x-1)-2(x-1)=0$ \\
$(x-1)(3x-2)=0$ \\
$(x-1)=0$ or $(3x-2)=0$ \\
\textbf{x = 1} or \textbf{x = 2/3} \\
\\\\\\

3) $x^2+5x+6=0$ \\
Solution: \\
$x^2+2x+3x+6=0$ \\
$x(x+2)+3(x+2)=0$ \\
$(x+2)(x+3)=0$ \\
$(x+2)=0$ or $(x+3)=0$ \\
\textbf{x = -2} or \textbf{x = -3} \\
\\\\\\
